vijay

Vijay played the lead role for the first time in Naalaiya Theerpu at the age of 18 in 1992.[12] He then appeared in the films Senthoorapandi, Rasigan, Deva and Coimbatore Mappillai, most of those were successful.[13][14] In 1996, Vijay acted in Poove Unakkaga, directed by Vikraman, which according to him, gave him his initial break and popularity.[15] In 1997, Vijay acted in Kaalamellam Kaathiruppen and Love Today.
